Caesar Cipher App

A Caesar cipher is a simple encryption device. It works by substituting each letter of a message with another, predetermined letter. The substitutions are decided by shifting the position in the alphabet a fixed number of 'jumps' - with a key of 2, 'a' becomes 'c', 'b' becomes 'd' and so on.

Enter your text below, choose a key (this must be a number) and hit encrypt. To decrypt text that you have the key for, paste it into the box, enter the key and press 'decrypt'.
